How CRISPR Gene Therapy Works

1

Collect Cells

Stem cells are collected from the patient's bone marrow through a standard medical procedure.

2

Gene Editing

CRISPR technology precisely edits the BCL11A gene, reactivating fetal hemoglobin production.

3

Conditioning

Patient receives conditioning treatment to prepare bone marrow for the edited cells.

4

Cell Infusion

Gene-edited stem cells are infused back into the patient through a simple IV procedure.

5

Healthy Blood

Edited cells produce healthy red blood cells with high levels of fetal hemoglobin.
