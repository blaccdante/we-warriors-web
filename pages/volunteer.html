Volunteer Opportunities

Find the perfect way to contribute your time and talents

Peer Support

Provide emotional support and encouragement to warriors and families through one-on-one mentoring and group sessions.

Requirements & Skills:

  • Compassionate and empathetic nature
  • Excellent listening and communication skills
  • Complete training provided - no experience needed
4 hours per month

Education & Outreach

Help educate communities Living with Sickle Cell disease through presentations, workshops, and awareness campaigns.

Requirements & Skills:

  • Public speaking experience preferred
  • Passion for education and helping others learn
  • All training materials and resources provided
6 hours per month

Event Support

Help organize and run community events, fundraisers, and awareness campaigns that bring warriors together.

Requirements & Skills:

  • Event planning experience helpful but not required
  • Strong team collaboration and leadership skills
  • Flexible schedule for event dates and times
Flexible schedule

Digital Support

Assist with website updates, social media management, and digital communications to expand our reach.

Requirements & Skills:

  • Basic computer and internet navigation skills
  • Familiarity with social media platforms
  • Creative mindset and eye for engaging content
3 hours per week

Medical Support

Healthcare professionals can volunteer their expertise for medical advice, screenings, and educational content.

Requirements & Skills:

  • Medical background or healthcare training required
  • Current professional license or certification
  • Sickle cell knowledge preferred but training available
4 hours per month

Creative Services

Use your creative talents for graphic design, photography, videography, and content creation for our campaigns.

Requirements & Skills:

  • Portfolio showcasing creative work and style
  • Proficiency in design software (Photoshop, Canva, etc.)
  • Creative vision aligned with our mission
5 hours per month
