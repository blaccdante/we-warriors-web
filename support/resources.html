Insurance Navigation & Coverage

üîç Understanding Your Insurance Coverage

‚úÖ What Should Be Covered

  • Regular hematologist visits
  • Hydroxyurea and other disease-modifying medications
  • Pain management medications
  • Emergency room visits for pain crises
  • Blood transfusions when medically necessary
  • Preventive vaccinations
  • Annual screening tests (TCD, echocardiogram)

üìû Insurance Navigation Tips

  1. Know Your Policy: Request a copy of your benefits summary
  2. Pre-Authorization: Understand which treatments need approval
  3. In-Network Providers: Find sickle cell specialists in your network
  4. Appeal Process: Know how to appeal denied claims
  5. Document Everything: Keep records of all medical communications
