Counseling Services

Professional support for your emotional and spiritual journey

Comprehensive Support for Warriors & Families

Living with sickle cell disease presents unique emotional, spiritual, and psychological challenges. Our counseling services provide professional support tailored to the warrior journey, helping individuals and families navigate the complexities of chronic illness with strength and hope.

  • Prayer and meditation guidance

Specialized Support Areas

Crisis Support

24/7 crisis intervention and emotional support during pain crises and medical emergencies.

Grief & Loss

Support for processing losses related to health, lifestyle changes, and life transitions.

Relationship Counseling

Help navigating relationships, dating, marriage, and intimacy with chronic illness.

Career & Life Planning

Guidance on career decisions, education, and life planning with sickle cell disease.
