Why Join a Support Group?

Support groups provide a safe space where you can connect with others who truly understand the sickle cell journey. Whether you're newly diagnosed, a long-time warrior, or a caring family member, you'll find community, understanding, and practical support.

Ready to Connect?

Taking the first step to join a support group can feel overwhelming, but you're not alone. Here's how to get started:

1

Choose Your Format

Decide if you prefer in-person, online, or both types of support

2

Contact the Facilitator

Reach out to introduce yourself and ask any questions

3

Attend Your First Meeting

Come as you are - everyone understands it's a big step

4

Give It Time

It may take a few sessions to feel comfortable - that's normal
