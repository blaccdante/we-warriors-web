Community Guidelines

‚úÖ What We Encourage

  • Share your experiences and insights
  • Ask questions - no question is too small
  • Offer support and encouragement
  • Celebrate victories, big and small
  • Provide helpful resources and tips
  • Respect different perspectives and experiences

üö´ What We Don't Allow

  • Specific medical advice (share experiences, not prescriptions)
  • Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
  • Spam or promotional content
  • Sharing personal medical information of others
  • Political discussions unrelated to sickle cell advocacy
  • Discouraging medical treatment or spreading misinformation

üí° Tips for Great Discussions

  • Use clear titles: Help others find relevant discussions
  • Share context: Age, sickle cell type, location can help
  • Be patient: Not everyone checks daily
  • Search first: Your question might already be discussed
  • Use content warnings: For sensitive topics
  • Thank contributors: Show appreciation for helpful responses

üÜò Need Help?

  • Technical issues: Contact support@wewarriors.org
  • Report inappropriate content: Use the report button
  • Moderation questions: Message the moderator team
  • Crisis support: Call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • Medical emergencies: Call 911 immediately
